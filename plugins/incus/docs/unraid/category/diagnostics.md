[## 📄️Capture diagnostics and logs
When you have issues with your Unraid server, gathering detailed information is crucial for effective troubleshooting. This information helps others provide accurate and timely assistance, especially when you post in forums.

%%UDMA|udma%% %%CRC errors|crc-errors%% (SMART attribute 199) are typical for Unraid users and often appear in drive health reports. For most users, these errors indicate a communication problem between the drive and the server rather than a failure of the drive itself.
